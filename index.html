Text Styles

Heading H1

  • text-align: center
  • letter-spacing: 4px
  • line-height: 80px
  • word-spacing: 5px

Heading H4

  • text-align: center
  • font-weight: lighter
  • text-decoration-line: underline
  • text-decoration-style: double

Heading H6

  • text-align: right
  • font-weight: lighter
  • margin-right: 50px

Paragraph p

  • text-align: justify
  • word-spacing: 5px
